About ProTech Assist (SL) Limited

Empowering Schools Through Technology

Our Vision

ProTech Assist (SL) Limited is a technology company focused on building digital solutions that simplify operations for institutions across Sierra Leone.

What We Do

We specialize in developing secure and reliable platforms that help schools manage student admissions, track payments, and organize data efficiently.

"Our flagship platform provides a centralized system where multiple schools can register, receive student applications online, and monitor payments—including those made through agents—without confusion."

Our Mission

At ProTech Assist, our mission is to digitize and improve the education sector by providing tools that are simple, affordable, and effective. We are committed to helping schools transition from manual processes to smart digital systems.
